# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

The Impact Collaboration Platform (ICP) is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## 1. Information We Collect

*   **Account Information**: When you log in (via Google), we store your name, email address, and profile picture provided by the authentication provider.
*   **User-Generated Content**: We store data you submit, such as Solutions, Partner profiles, and Tickets.
*   **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the platform.

## 2. How We Use Information

*   **Service Provision**: To facilitate connections between partners and solutions (e.g., displaying your contact info on a Solution you own).
*   **Communication**: To notify you about status updates on your submissions (e.g., tickets).
*   **AI Features**:
    *   **Data Processing**: The AI features (Chat, Extraction) process the text you provide to generate responses or structure data.
    *   **No Training**: We do **not** use your private data to train the foundational AI models.
    *   **Human-in-the-Loop**: AI suggestions for data entry are always reviewed by a human before being saved to the database.

## 3. Data Sharing

*   **Public Data**: "Mature" Solutions and Partners are visible to the public (including anonymous users) to further the goal of connection.
*   **Private Data**: Contact details may be restricted based on user roles.
*   **Third Parties**: We use Google Cloud Platform (Firebase, Vertex AI) as our infrastructure provider.

## 4. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your account and data.
